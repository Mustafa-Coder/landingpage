Turki Al Alalshikh

Turki bin Abdul Mohsen bin Abdul Latif Al Sheikh (August 4, 1981) - Board of Directors of the General Entertainment Authority. Al Sheikh has held several sports positions, President of the Football Association, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the General Sports Authority in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Chairman of the Saudi Olympic Committee, Chairman of the Board of Directors and Chairman of the Board of Directors. Honorary members of the Board of Directors of Al-Wehda Club in Makkah Al-Mukarramah, and the former owner of the Egyptian Pyramids Club. Turki Al-Sheikh was famous on the artistic level through his lyrical poetic writings, and he was sung by Gulf and Arab artists.
